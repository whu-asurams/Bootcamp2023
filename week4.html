Week 4 (July 10- July 14)

General Description: During the fourth week, you will design a website for yourself. You decide what you want to show on your website. Especially, you will choose a particular topic, such as documents that supports your college applications, or a topic that you are interested in, such as your favorite sports, your favorite music. Determine what to include on each of your webpage. Now let us make them happen.

Theme of the week: Your well designed website with at least four webpages.
